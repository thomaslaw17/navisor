About Us


Hong Kong has long been praised as a “shopping haven” and is famous for its numerous tourist attractions. Through engaging in the local trips from traditional package tour, travellers usually visit popular travellers spots like Tsim Sha Tsui and Mongkok for shopping, the Peak for sightseeing and Ocean Park for recreating. Yet, they lack the opportunities to explore the other sides of Hong Kong, for example the natural scenery, local cuisines, heritage and nightlife.

We endeavor to provide travellers around the world with customized local trips where they will have a brand new experience in exploration of the fantasy in Hong Kong. With our young, energetic “navigators” who are aged from 18 to 40, travellers can indulge themselves in the unique culture and beauty of Hong Kong and act like a local!

Interested in being a Hong Kong local for one day or some days? With Navisor, you can customize your own local trips simply by browsing our website on your phone or laptop. Time, budget, theme of the trip, etc. can all be under your control. Having no idea of where to visit, what to experience in Hong Kong? Our “navigators” could offer the most authentic travelling itineraries to you.
